04 THE PROTOCOL — SIX DIRECTIVES TO RAISE THE SIGNAL

01

PICK ONE LANE. MAKE THE REST SUPPORTING.

Choose "Brandon the Wizard — visionary artist" as the hero pillar (paintings + persona + music as proof of the world). Aim ~70% of posts there. Everything else (comics, thrift, pendants) becomes occasional flavor, not co-headliners.

▸ WHY: A focused feed lets Instagram find your tribe and show you to lookalikes. Scatter keeps reach cold.
02

HOOK ON STAKES, NOT ANNOUNCEMENTS.

Kill "now on all platforms." Open with a first-person stake or a curiosity gap: "I painted this in one sitting and it scared me a little —" · "They don't want you buying comics from these 3 publishers. Here's why." Win the first 1 second.

▸ WHY: Your best posts (tribute, conspiracy take) already had stakes. Make that the rule, not the exception.
03

EVERY POST MUST EARN A SAVE OR A SHARE.

Build in a reason to keep it: a technique reveal, a hot take worth arguing, a "save this for later," a quotable line. Saves + shares are the rocket fuel the algorithm amplifies — likes barely move the needle.

▸ WHY: The account's reach ceiling is low because almost nothing is being saved or sent to a friend.
04

STOP DUMPING THE AUDIOBOOK. REPACKAGE IT.

Don't post Ijia chapter-by-chapter as raw reels. Cut one high-impact trailer (best art + a cliffhanger line + your mom's narration) and pin it. Drive to the full audiobook via link. One great post beats ten dead ones.

▸ WHY: A run of 1-like clips tells the algorithm the whole account is low-interest and suppresses your good posts.
05

LEAD WITH THE PAINTINGS AS REELS.

Your psychedelic art is your strongest visual — but it's posted as still photos. Film the process: time-lapse the canvas, hands moving, paint hitting in the first second. Art process is some of the most saved/shared content on the platform.

▸ WHY: Strong visual in second 1 = the single biggest driver of watch-time and reach.
06

FEWER, STRONGER. QUALITY OVER FLOOD.

Drop from 3–6 posts/day to 1 strong post/day (or even 4–5 great ones a week). Put the effort that went into 6 throwaway clips into one reel with a real hook, clean visual, and a save-worthy payoff.

▸ WHY: The algorithm rewards your average post quality. Flooding lowers it; concentration raises it.
